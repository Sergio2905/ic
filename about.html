Our Founders’ Jorney

IC Assemblies’ story began with Al Gomez, who immigrated from Cuba to the U.S. in 1961 amidst the communist revolution. Al’s unwavering dedication led him to serve in the U.S. Army and work in manufacturing since 1970. In 1982, he founded Industrial Components from the garage of his West Miami home, laying the groundwork for what would become IC Assemblies.

In 1991, responding to the needs of his manufacturers and to help support domestic manufacturing jobs from an outsourcing wave, Al expanded his vision by establishing a contract manufacturing facility in the Dominican Republic.

Joining him in this venture was Lucy Gomez, a seasoned entrepreneur who sold her successful metal stamping company in 1989 to become the full-time CFO at IC Assemblies. Together, Al and Lucy built a strong foundation for the company, driven by their shared commitment to honesty, innovation and quality.

Electro mechanical assemblies

Client Profile:

Our client is a leading maritime engineering company specializing in the design and manufacture of high-performance propellers for commercial and military vessels worldwide. With a reputation for innovation and quality, they sought to optimize their manufacturing process to meet the increasing demand for efficient and durable propellers in the maritime industry.

Challenge:

The client faced several challenges in their propeller manufacturing process, including:

  1. Complex Design Requirements: Maritime propellers require intricate designs tailored to specific vessel types and operating conditions, leading to challenges in precision manufacturing.
  2. Material Selection: Choosing the right materials for propeller construction is crucial for durability, corrosion resistance, and performance optimization.
  3. Production Efficiency: With growing demand and competition, the client aimed to streamline their manufacturing process to improve efficiency without compromising quality.

Solution:

To address these challenges, our team collaborated closely with the client to implement the following solutions:

  1. Advanced Design Optimization: Leveraging cutting-edge CAD/CAM software, we conducted thorough design analysis and optimization to ensure that each propeller met the client's performance requirements while minimizing material waste and production time.
  2. Material Expertise: Through extensive research and testing, we recommended the use of high-strength marine-grade alloys known for their superior mechanical properties and corrosion resistance, enhancing the longevity and performance of the propellers.
  3. Process Refinement: We conducted a comprehensive review of the client's manufacturing processes, identifying areas for improvement and implementing lean manufacturing principles to streamline production workflows and reduce lead times.

Results:

The implementation of our solutions yielded significant improvements for the client:

  1. Enhanced Performance: The optimized propeller designs resulted in improved hydrodynamic efficiency, reduced fuel consumption, and enhanced maneuverability for vessels equipped with our client's propellers.
  2. Quality Assurance: By utilizing high-quality materials and precision manufacturing techniques, the client achieved exceptional product quality and reliability, earning trust and recognition from maritime operators worldwide.
  3. Operational Efficiency: The streamlined manufacturing process led to increased production capacity and reduced production costs, allowing the client to meet growing market demand while maintaining competitive pricing.

Conclusion:

Through collaboration, innovation, and expertise, our client successfully overcame manufacturing challenges to deliver cutting-edge maritime propellers that set new standards for performance and reliability in the industry. By continuously striving for excellence, they solidified their position as a trusted partner for maritime propulsion solutions, driving efficiency and sustainability across the seas.
